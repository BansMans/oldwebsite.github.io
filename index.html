History

Math and Physics have been my strong suit throughout my early education. I decided I wanted to try the Mechanical Engineering Technology program at Camosun after high school because of my affinity for building and problem solving, as well as my interest in robotics. After some work in the field, I decided to finish my education in Engineering at UVic and received my Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering in 2023. I've worked in Crofton at Geotech Industries, in the Esquimalt Dockyard with Babcock Canada and currently working in the manufacturing shop Rainhouse Manufacturing Canada.

I am British Columbia-born-and-raised, and I have lived in Victoria for the majority of my life. Disregard the fact that I'm posing in front of Niagara Falls below.
